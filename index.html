ABOUT THE INSTITUTION
R.M.K. Engineering College, started in the year 1995, has been achieving Academic Excellence for the past 25 years. Our Institution has always been in one among the top 10 positions among all the Engineering colleges affiliated to Anna Uni-versity and also has maintained excellent placement record. Our college received autonomous status in the academic year 2020-21. In 2008, the College was conferred Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan National Award for its Overall Performance followed by the "Best Principal " Award in the year 2012, by the Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) New Delhi. Our College is accredited by NAAC with A+ Grade for 5 years and NAAC has approved our Institution to mentor 10 institutions for NAAC accreditation. Presently, the college offers 9 under-graduate programs and 3 post-graduate programs and 7 eligible UG programmes are accredited by NBA. Our Institution is ranked 155th in the NIRF ranking under the Engineering insti-tutes category in the year 2019 and was ranked 6th across the country in SWACHHTA 2017 ranking by MHRD, Govt. of India. The College has been awarded five-star rating under Institution Innovation Council (IIC) MIC, MHRD, Govt. of India. ABOUT THE DEPARTMENTS
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering established in the year 1997, Provides an outstanding research environment complemented by excellence in teaching. The Department offers an under-graduate program B.E.(CSE) with an annual intake to 180 students, a post-graduate programme M.E. (CSE) since 2004 and Ph.D. since 2006. The B.E. program has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in the year 2006 and again in 2013 and 2018 respectively. 30 Research Scholars have been awarded Ph.D. degree under the research centre so far. The students of the Department have been consistently receiving Anna University Ranks and Medals and have won Cognizant Big Idea Contest, Smart India Hackathons, TCS Best Project Awards and other National Level Awards and Recognitions. The Department has also established five industry collaborated Centers of Excellence (COEs) like Artificial Intelligence COE, Bigdata COE, Information Security COE, Cloud Computing COE and Front-End Engineering COE. The Department has also received R&D funds from organizations like AICTE and IEI. The Department has received AICTE-CII award for Best Industry linked Computer & IT Engineering institute in the year 2018 under Platinum category and CSI best student chapter award during the year 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 consecutively.

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in the academic year 1995 and has annual intake of 180 students and has M.E Program in Applied Electronics. The B.E. program has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in the year 2006, and again in 2013 and 2018 respectively. The department received AICTE-CII survey award 2015 for the best industry linked Electronics and Allied institute (All India 1 strank) and Dr.Amitabha Bhattacharyya Memorial Trophy" presented by "The Institution of Engineers (India) [IEI]" as the "Third Best Engineering College Students' Chapter for the Year 2018-2019" The Department has well equipped laborato-ries i.e, Digital Design, Digital Signal Processing, Microprocessor, Analog & Digital Communications, Electronic Circuits, RF & Microwave and VLSI Design which cater to program needs. Adequate numbers of computers with required software and EDA tools are available in the laboratory. The Department has extensive and fruitful interactions with the industry, R & D organizations and other professional bodies (ISTE, IEEE, IETE, IACSIT), the interactions with them culminate into professional aaivities, research agendas and partnerships through MOUs. The Department has con-ducted a National Level FDP in the thrust area of Telemedicine under the AICTE Training and Learning (ATAL) Academy Program in 2020 - 2021. Our dept. received funded projects worth Rs. 1,02,05,000/- from AICTE, DST and other industries. The department has established the centre of Excellences (CoE) in Telecom and Robotic Process automa-tion in association with the industries to provide training and develop products in latest technologies.

The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 1999. The Department offers B.Tech Infor-mation Technology Program with an intake of 60. The B.Tech Information Technology Program has been granted accredi-tation by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for three years. R.M.K. Engineering College has collaborated with Virtusa Software Services Private Limited for a new initiative called Virtusa Batch Adoption Program. The Department has received AICTE-CII Award 2018 for the Best Industry-Linked Institute for Computer & IT Engineering (Established-Degree). The department has well equipped state-of-the-art labora-tory facilities to carry out various projects, research and consultancy activities. A dedicated team of faculty members con-tributes to the holistic development of students and maintains high standards of teaching and learning. All the faculty members of the Department are members of various professional societies and technical bodies like IEEE, ACM, CSI, ISTE, IEI etc.
